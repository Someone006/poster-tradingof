Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This policy explains how poster tradingof handles data when preparing and publishing authorized short-form video clips.

1. Information We Process

The service may process information needed to operate connected publishing workflows, including:

  • Basic account information provided by authorized third-party platforms, such as profile identifier, display name, and avatar.
  • Video files, titles, captions, hashtags, publishing status, and platform upload responses.
  • Authentication tokens granted through official OAuth authorization flows.
  • Technical logs needed to troubleshoot uploads and automation runs.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used to authenticate the authorized creator, prepare video clips, upload or publish content to connected accounts, prevent duplicate processing, and maintain reliable operation of the workflow.

3. Sharing

Information is shared with third-party platforms only as needed to provide the requested publishing features. The service does not sell personal information.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and workflow data should be stored securely and limited to the systems required to run the service. Tokens should be revoked if access is no longer needed or if unauthorized access is suspected.

5. Data Retention

Workflow records and generated assets may be kept as long as needed for publishing, auditing, troubleshooting, or creator review. Data can be deleted when it is no longer needed.

6. User Control

You may revoke platform access at any time through the connected platform's account or app settings. Revoking access may stop automated upload and publishing features.

7. Children

The service is not directed to children and is intended for use by the authorized creator or account operator.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact
